Sleep App

Allow yourself to drift off to sleep with this white noise application! This application provides a number of different white noise tracks to choose from, so try it out and see which fits you best. Mix your own soundscapes by changing the volume on several different sound tracks. You can continue to use other applications on your phone while white noise plays in the background.

How To Use

The application has two main screens:
  • Tracks
  • Profiles
On the Tracks screen toggle each track on and off with the toggle control, and change the volume with the slider. On the Profiles screen you may save track profiles under a specified name. To overwrite a profile enter the name of an existing profile in the box and click the Save Profile Button.

On the Tracks screen there is a Mute button on the top right of the screen.

The sounds will continue to play in the background while you do other tasks.
