GeoTalk

Leave messages for your friends, or notes about a location you want others to see with this app. Look for messages in your surrounding area with the augmented reality screen.

How To Use

Privacy Policy

This application is limitless! You can leave messages detailing interesting information about a location, create a geographical scavenger hunt, socialize with friends in a new and interesting way, and more. To enter a new message go to the second tab type your message in the box at the top and press "Send". There are three different ways to view messages around you. The first is to use the augmented reality view on the first tab to view the messages around you in their locations relative to you. You may click on the message to get a more stable look at the message in an alert box. On the second tab you can see a list of messages close to you, sorted by distance. The third tab is a Google Maps view with markers in the geographic locations. Click on the markers to view the messages. The GPS accuracy is a typically 5 meter radius. Some messages may appear to jump as your location is calibrated or may seem out of place because of the variance in accuracy.
